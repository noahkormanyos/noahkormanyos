My Story

My passion for technology has inspired me to make a career out of solving challenging business problems with specific focus on statistical analysis.

From a young age I was aware of my keen fascination for solving problems. As I've grown older, I've exhibited this desire in a variety of academic and extra-curricular settings.

Starting my own business in 2018 has arguably been one of my most valuable professional experiences to date. Filing for an LLC, acquiring clients, and conducting professional business meetings are just some of the invaluable professional skills I've learned throughout this process.

In the time since creating my digital marketing agency, I've deeply expanded my knowledge in areas such as; statistics, data analysis, and economics. The exploration of these areas, combined with my internal passion for problem solving has helped me confidently decide to pursue a career in data science.

With respect to my academic degree at Clarkson University, I plan to graduate this December [2020] with a B.S. in Mathematical-Economics. This portfolio serves to demonstrate my data science knowledge and applicable problem solving skills.
